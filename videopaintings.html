VIDEO PAINTINGS

An abnormal happening, a dejected form, an abjected presence. A circuitry of the present in evolution, of a chroma-cosmic amorphous signal, always becoming, in each and each passing, in trace of its roots.


Agâh (turkish)/(meaning); knowing, foresighted, aware.
The image swirling, detached from the habitual, but in narration, of affect and sensation.
Transcending state, permeating matter, the organism expands into a phenomen.

The works here are aimed to create a zone of immanence which question the finite properties of the medium, and contemplate on the temporal and spatial aesthetics of detachment, and pleasure.
